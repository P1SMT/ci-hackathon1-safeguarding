Adult Safeguarding: Protecting Vulnerable Adults Understanding Adult Safeguarding Adult safeguarding is a crucial aspect of creating a society where everyone feels safe and protected. It involves taking steps to prevent abuse and neglect, and to ensure that the rights and well-being of vulnerable adults are upheld. Vulnerable adults may include the elderly, individuals with disabilities, those with mental health issues, and anyone who may be at risk due to their circumstances.
